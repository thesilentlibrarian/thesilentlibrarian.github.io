ROX Repeated Refine Simulator

Jul 28 2021

Summary#

This page contains the refinement simulator for Ragnarok X. In this game, refining equipment has a variable chance to upgrade, failed, damage, and even downgrade. This simulator helps conduct a controlled experiment on this refinement mechanism, producing a report that may or may not be valuable for making refinement decisions inside the game.

For step-by-step refinement simulator, click here.

Simulator#

Input Parameters#

To use the simulator, please input the experiment parameters. Then click the “Run Simulation” button.

*Price for Enriched Oridecon and HD Oridecon will be derived. Enriched price is 4x, HD is 16x.

How many times should you refine to reach the target level?

From the simulation, we can conclude the following statements:

  • If you are the average guy with normal luck, you can see the p50. During the simulation, 50% of the experiment reach the target level after X refinements.
  • If you have a tendency with slightly bad luck, you can see the p75. During the simulation, 75% of the experiment reach the target level after X refinements.
  • If you are pessimistic and have super horrible bad luck, you can see the p95. During the simulation, 95% of the experiment reach the target level after X refinements.
